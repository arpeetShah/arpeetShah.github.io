## Tips
- **Landscape (16:10-ish) works best** — tiles are cropped to fill.
- Phone photos are fine. **Darker, moodier shots match this site best.**
- Aim for ~1600px wide. Keep files under ~500KB so the site stays fast.
- Screenshots: use `Cmd + Shift + 4`, then Space, then click the window.
